+ Important Information

Necromancer Shields:

  • Necro Shields will roll up to THREE +1-3 Skill Bonuses
  • Non Unique/Set Necro Heads will always have Added Poison Damage

Paladin Shields:

  • Paladin Shields will roll +All Resist OR +Attack Rating & Enhanced Damage
  • Max Possible All Resist (Affix Lvl 50+): 45
  • Max Possible +Attack Rating (Affix Lvl 50+): 121
  • Max Possible Enhanced Damage (Affix Lvl 50+): 65%

Misc:

  • Max Sockets are only possible for items with i-lvl > 40
  • Damage Values for Boots/Shields are NOT improved when Ethereal
