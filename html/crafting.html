Crafting Recipes

+ Important Information
  • Crafted items will NEVER be Ethereal, even if using an Ethereal item in the Recipe
  • Preset Affixes STACK with any of the same Affixes rolled. The Max possible value is in brackets for each Affix
  • If a Crafted Item has a final i-lvl > 70, it will always roll an additional 4 Affixes
  • Amulets must have a Final Affix lvl of at least 90 in order to roll +2 Class Skills
  • Amulets & Rings have a Q-lvl of 1.
